Deviled Eggs in Honor of My Father

  • 12 Large Eggs
  • Crisp crumbled bacon
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 T yellow mustard
  • 1/4 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • Sweet paprika

Cook bacon until crisp then blot dry on paper towels. Mince bacon in crumbles. Set aside. Fill a large saucepan with water and bring to a boil. With a slotted spoon slowly lower eggs one at a time into the water. Reduce the heat to a low boil and cook for 10 minutes. Drain. Put eggs in an ice bath to cool then remove shells.

Slice each eg in half lengthwise. Remove the yolks and place them in a bowl. Arrange the whites cut-side up on a serving platter. To the bowl add the mayo, mustard, bacon, salt pepper. Mash and mix with fork until smooth (you can use a small food processor if you have one

Yield: 24 pieces
