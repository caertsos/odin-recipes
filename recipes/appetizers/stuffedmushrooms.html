Auntie Susie's Stuffed Mushrooms

  • 16 extra0large white Mushrooms
  • 5 tbsp good olive oil, divided
  • 2 1/2 tbsp Marsala wine or medium Sherry
  • 3/4 pound sweet Italian sausage, removed from casings
  • 6 scallion, white and green parts, minced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2/3 cup panko crumbs
  • 5 oz. Mascarpone cheese, preferably from Italy
  • 1/3 cup freshly grated Parmesan
  • 2 1/2 tbsp fresh parsley, minced
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
